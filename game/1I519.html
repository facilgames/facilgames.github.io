    Skibidi Retro FPS

Explore a maze-like world, collect coins, and defeat Skibidi to advance to the next level. Your main goal in the game is to collect the required number of coins in each stage before the skibidi get you. Use your shotgun to shoot the bad guys and stay alive. Be careful, though. You only have a limited number of bullets, so make every shot count! How to play the game For this challenge, you will need both your computer mouse and keyboard. The mouse is used to aim and shoot your weapon, while the keyboard is used to move your character. Here are your controls: - W: Move forward - S: Move backward. - A and D: Strafe left and right. To complete each level and advance to the next one, you must find all the coins scattered throughout the maze corridors. However, evil skibidi can pop out of nowhere, so always be on the lookout. Use your aiming skills and put all of them down with your shotgun! At the same time, always keep an eye on your life points. Whenever you get hit by an enemy, you'll lose a small percentage of your life. When it reaches 0, it's game over. The ammo is also limited. So keep that in mind when pressing the trigger. You don't want to run out of ammo in the middle of a fight. What else you should know The good part is you can also find some power-ups that you can use to your advantage. Look for these: - Green Mushrooms: These will replenish your life points. - Ammo boxes: Collect those so you can keep shooting the attacking foes. Each level will be more challenging than the last, so make sure you stay alert and keep your wits about you. Overall, Skibidi FPS online game provides a thrilling challenge that will keep you on your toes. So grab your shotgun, get ready to shoot some skibidi, and let's go save the Mushroom Kingdom!
